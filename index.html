About

Mayur Siwan is a Hindustani classical vocalist based in Delhi NCR, India. Trained in the traditional guru-shishya parampara, his music reflects depth, discipline, and emotional sincerity.

Repertoire & Training

  • Khayal, Bhajan, Classical and semi-classical forms
  • Training in Gwalior gharana
  • Focus on raga-based improvisation

Versatile Range of Programs

  • Kirtans
  • Bollywood
  • AOL Satsangs
  • Prayer Meetings
  • Private Gigs
  • Musical Pheras
